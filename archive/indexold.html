About Me

My name is Jemmin (IPA: dʒεmɪn). Proudly hailing from the quaint town of Somers in Westchester County, NY, I moved to Pittsburgh (pictured above) last year to study computer science at Carnegie Mellon University.

My academic interests include programming, theoretical computer science, artificial intelligence, and linguistics. Some of my work can be seen below.

I play viola and violin, and I'm a big classical music fan - I've been lucky to always live close to where many of the greatest musicians in the world perform. And I'm always looking for concert buddies, so feel free to get in touch!

I also enjoy tennis and ping pong, competitive SCRABBLE, camping, hiking, and making ice cream.
